Site Plan: Lagos Laundry Express

Domain Suggestion: lagoslaundryexpress.com (optional)

Site Name

Lagos Laundry Express – This name reflects the fast and reliable laundry service based in Lagos, Nigeria. It emphasizes convenience, locality, and speed of delivery, which is important to our target market.

Site Purpose

The website will provide a platform for customers to learn about laundry services, book pickups and deliveries, and contact the business. It will include pricing, service options, customer testimonials, and a FAQ section.

Scenarios

  • How can I schedule a laundry pickup from my home or office?
  • What are the pricing options for express and regular laundry services?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color – Navy Blue (#002D72): Used for headers and the navigation bar.
  • Accent Color – Light Sky Blue (#87CEFA): Used for background accents and call-to-action buttons.

Typography

  • Heading Font – Montserrat: Used for all headers to give a clean and modern feel.
  • Body Font – Open Sans: Used for paragraph text for readability and accessibility.
